About US

At GRME, we develop brands for you to help you interact with your internal and external audiences to amplify your business. Be it short-form video or long-form documentary, real-time editorial. From an experiential activation, to a fully integrated marketing campaign and even ongoing influencer programs, to paid media partnerships. GRME's team of award-winning strategists, designers, writers, researchers and technologists are here to do more than make your story come to life. We nourish the growth of your brand, product or service in the right direction with intelligence and originality. Creating concepts based on relevant insights of your target audience and developing strategies expressed in social media, audiovisual pieces, illustrations, interactive games or any other medium; always thinking in creating business opportunities for our valued customers.
